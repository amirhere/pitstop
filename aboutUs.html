What Makes us Different

We follow a unique approach which we refer to as the EDT i.e. Evaluate, Design and Train. Taking a leap from the quintessential trainings where for a single day session organizations have to pay through their nose and usually the return on investment is very minimal.


We at The Pit Stop work hand in hand with enterprises. It is a consistent, constant and continuous enabling process whereby we evaluate an employee for their core skills and any gap or weakness that he/she might have. This spans across all the functions of an organization i.e. Sales, Finance, Supply Chain, Administration etc. Once the evaluation process is completed we customize and design the training accordingly to suit the personality of the employees involved. This makes our approach unique and focused as the employee is delivered what he requires the most.


Our Vision

We want to be capacity enablers for individuals from all walks of life. Our aim is to empower people so they realize their true potential to lead a successful life. We endorse Self Actualization.
